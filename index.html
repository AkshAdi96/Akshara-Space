Akshara

Some feelings outlive the moments that created them.

I built this space because sometimes words unsaid take up the most room in my mind. I needed to put this out into the universe to let you know how much I miss you, and how deeply I still care.

The quiet moments, your laugh, the way the world just makes a little more sense when you are around—I miss all of it. As the poet E.E. Cummings beautifully wrote, "I carry your heart with me (I carry it in my heart)."

This isn't a goodbye. It is simply me leaving a light on in the window, hoping you know that the space you hold in my life will always remain yours.

I needed you to know... I still love you. And I miss you so much.
